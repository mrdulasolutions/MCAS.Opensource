# Disclaimers

## This is not medical advice.

This repository contains **hypothesis-generation tooling** and **in silico
predictions** — computational guesses about which compounds might affect MCAS /
MCAD biology. **None of it is validated for human use.** Nothing here:

- diagnoses any condition,
- prescribes any treatment,
- recommends any dose, route, or schedule, or
- claims that any compound (pharma, herb, or supplement) is safe or effective
  for any individual.

If you have MCAS / MCAD or suspect you do, work with a clinician experienced in
mast cell disease. The Mastocytosis Society (TMSforacure.org) maintains a list
of mast-cell-knowledgeable specialists.

## Do not self-experiment.

Even compounds listed here as "natural" or "non-controlled" can:
- interact dangerously with prescription drugs (e.g., quercetin inhibits several
  CYP450 enzymes; sulforaphane affects phase II detox; resveratrol affects
  warfarin metabolism),
- be contraindicated in pregnancy, kidney disease, liver disease, autoimmune
  conditions, and many other contexts,
- trigger MCAS flares (irony noted),
- mask symptoms while underlying disease progresses, or
- contain contaminants, mis-labeled actives, or fillers that cause harm.

**MCAS patients in particular are at elevated risk of reacting to new supplements.**

## In vitro results do not predict human response.

The wet-lab assays referenced in this repo (LAD2 β-hexosaminidase release, CD63
flow, tryptase ELISA) are screening tools, not approval pathways. A compound that
suppresses degranulation in a cell line may not do anything in a human, or may
do harm.

## Generated molecules are computational only.

REINVENT 4 produces novel SMILES. These are **untested chemical hypotheses**,
not drugs. Many will be impossible to synthesize, unstable, toxic, or
ineffective. Treat any generative output as a starting point for medicinal
chemistry and rigorous preclinical testing, never as a candidate for human use.

## Open-source is not the same as safe-to-use.

We publish predictions openly so that **researchers** can validate, falsify, or
extend them — and so that no finding gets patent-locked away from patients. It
does not mean any prediction is ready to act on.

## Reporting harm

If you believe this repository is propagating dangerous information, open an
issue tagged `safety` immediately. We treat safety reports as the highest
priority.
